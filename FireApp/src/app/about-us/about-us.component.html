ABOUT BOOSTED

What began as a few grad students looking for a better way to get around campus, has since grown to become a globally recognized brand on a mission to reinvent transportation. Boosted empowers people everywhere to move across their cities, campuses, and communities in ways that are fun, fast, and simple. We started with the world’s best electric skateboard, but our broader mission has always been to address the “last mile” problem with ultra-light, high-performance electric vehicles that redefine what transportation can be.
